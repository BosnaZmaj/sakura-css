A 16-Year Journey to Better Budgeting

From frustrated user to determined founder - the story of Sakura Budget

2009

The Discovery

Indy discovers envelope budgeting through a simple desktop program. For the first time, personal finance makes sense. Everything clicks.

"Finally, a budgeting method that actually works with how my brain thinks about money."
2022

The Search

After 13 years of loyalty, the original desktop app shows its age. The search begins for a modern replacement - YNAB, Monarch, Copilot, Goodbudget. Nothing fits.

"They were either too complicated, abandoned envelope budgeting, or left me more confused than before."
2023

The Decision

After trying everything available, the answer becomes clear: build it yourself. As a cybersecurity expert and Senior Software Engineer at FIS Global, Indy has the skills. Multiple iterations follow - from Excel to standalone app to full web platform.

Security-First Design
Parent-Tested Simplicity
2025

The Alpha Stage

Three years of development culminate in a full web platform entering final alpha. Every feature tested against one standard: "If my parents can't figure it out easily, we're doing something wrong."

"No accounting degree required, no complicated tutorials - just simple, secure envelope budgeting that actually works."
2026

Beta & Beyond

Sakura Budget prepares for beta testing. Built from the ground up with security at every layer, designed to be intuitive enough that anyone can use it, and refined through years of real-world budgeting experience.

The Vision: Make envelope budgeting accessible to everyone, without compromising on security or simplicity.
