Our Story

Sakura Budget was born from a simple frustration: traditional budgeting tools felt overwhelming, complicated, and out of touch with real life. After years of struggling with spreadsheets and apps that promised everything but delivered stress, our founders decided to create something different.

Inspired by the Japanese philosophy of mindful simplicity - embodied by the delicate beauty of sakura blossoms - we set out to build a budgeting tool that would feel as natural as breathing. We wanted to strip away the complexity and create something that actually helps people build a healthy relationship with money.

Today, over 15,000 families trust Sakura Budget to manage their finances. But we're just getting started. Our mission remains the same: to make personal finance beautiful, simple, and accessible to everyone.
